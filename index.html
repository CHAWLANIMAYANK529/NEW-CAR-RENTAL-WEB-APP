Your Questions,
Answered.

What documents are needed? -
To book a self-drive car, you need a valid Driving License and an original Government ID proof (Aadhar Card or Passport). For international travelers, an International Driving Permit (IDP) is mandatory.
Is fuel included in the rent? +
No, fuel is not included. We follow a "Full-to-Full" policy. You get the car with a full tank and must return it with a full tank to avoid refueling charges.
What if the car breaks down? +
We offer 24/7 Roadside Assistance. In the rare event of a breakdown, just call our support line. We will arrange a mechanic or a replacement vehicle immediately.
Do you offer airport delivery? +
Absolutely! We provide doorstep delivery to Jaipur Airport, Railway Station, Hotels, and your home. Nominal delivery charges may apply based on distance.
