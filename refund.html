Refund Policy

Last Updated: 2026

1. Booking Cancellation

Customers can cancel their booking within the allowed time frame. Refund eligibility depends on how early the cancellation is made.

2. Refund Eligibility

• Full refund if cancelled 24 hours before booking time
• 50% refund if cancelled within 12–24 hours
• No refund for last-minute cancellations

3. No-Show Policy

If the customer fails to pick up the vehicle without prior notice, no refund will be issued.

4. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

5. Security Deposit Refund

The security deposit will be refunded after vehicle inspection, provided there is no damage or pending dues.

6. Deductions

Charges may be deducted for fuel shortage, damages, late return, or traffic violations.

7. Payment Gateway Charges

Any payment gateway or transaction charges may be non-refundable.
